Title: Managing Exam Stress
Category: Stress Management
Type: GUIDE
Visibility: public

Overview:
Exam stress is common, but it can be managed with a few simple habits. The goal is to reduce overwhelm and keep your routine steady during busy periods.

Practical Tips:
1. Break your study plan into smaller tasks.
2. Use 25-minute focus sessions with short breaks.
3. Sleep at regular times instead of staying up late to cram.
4. Drink water and eat balanced meals.
5. If anxiety feels intense, talk to someone you trust.

When to Seek Help:
If stress starts affecting sleep, appetite, concentration, or daily functioning for several days, contact a mental health professional or a trusted support service.
